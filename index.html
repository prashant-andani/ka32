About Us

KA32.in is a non-profit organization for Kalaburagi founded by a team of young and passionate IT professionals. KA32.in aims to build an all-in-one handy web portal to help Kalaburagi and web users by providing the Internet solutions for their daily needs. KA32.in also aims to provide a platform of service in promoting Tourism, strengthening Education by allowing access to the Quality and Standard e-Learning classroom based trainings for all courses and easy Real Estate solutions to sell / buy / rent properties.

KA32.in welcomes the passionate IT professionals who would like to contribute for the betterment of Kalaburagi society. Please do write your feedback or comments to contact@ka32.in Your valuable inputs does really help us grow and serve better.
